Edge of Light, 2020

25:45 | Watch on Youtube

Edge of Light was a light art exhibition curated by fellow artist Jonathan Sims for HoloCenter. I performed this a/v set for the exhibition opening, using an 8 foot spherical weather balloon as my projection surface. The opening performances were curated by Testu Collective, and the documentation was shot by Shintaro Ueyama.
