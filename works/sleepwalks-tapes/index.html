Sleepwalks was a solo project I released ambient casette tapes and played shows under from 2017-2018. I began recording Young & Ugly in the summer of 2016, when I spent a week in the forests of Oregon with a generator and a 1000-watt sound system inside a huge inflatable cube. Most of the tracks on these tapes were recorded while traveling, and they encompass the mood of a specific point in my life. The name appeared on many flyers for shows where I performed audiovisual sets during those years.
