Zine Club!

Interested in receiving my zines and/or mystery objects???

(It's basically my version of a P*treon, except payments go directly through Stripe.) Every subscription helps enable me to spend a little more time each month making weird art.

There are two tiers:

Tier 1: Quarterly E-zine

Receive a Quarterly Digital E-zine, downloadable and DRM-free, in your email inbox.
