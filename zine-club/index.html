Interested in receiving zines and/or mystery objects???

Zine Club is a thing where you can send me a small payment each month in exchange for getting cool stuff at regular intervals. (It's basically my version of a P*treon, except payments go directly through Stripe.) Every subscription helps enable me to spend a little more time each month making weird art.

There are two tiers:

Tier 1: Quarterly E-zine

Receive a Quarterly Digital E-zine, downloadable and DRM-free, in your email inbox.

  • Quarterly digital e-zine
  • Support weird art
Subscribe for $3/month

Tier 2: Bimonthly Mystery Object

Receive the Quarterly E-zine via email, and receive a Physical Mystery Object (print, sticker, and/or zine) via snail mail every other month.

  • Quarterly digital e-zine
  • Bimonthly mystery object
  • Support weird art
Subscribe for $7/month

How does it work?

Quarterly E-zines will be sent in February, May, August, and November.

Bimonthly Mystery Objects will be sent in February, April, June, August, October, and December.

Things will be sent out some time in the middle of the months listed above; exact timing can vary. Physical shipments are sent from New York via USPS, typically as a letter, postcard, or media mail, often without tracking. Shipping time will vary based on your location, but please allow a few weeks.

I accept international orders, and Stripe supports many currencies; however international shipping can take a long time, so please be patient if you live far away from me.

Your payment method will be charged each month on the same day you subscribed. You can change your address or payment method, switch tiers, or cancel at any time via the button below. Feel free to get in touch if you have any questions!
